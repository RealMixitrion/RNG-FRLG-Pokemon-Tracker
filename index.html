How to use this page

Save Entry — reads the pasted Ten Lines result automatically and saves it with your Hunting or Hitting choice.
Use Last Calibration — fills in the most recently saved ms and Advances calibration values so you do not need to type them again.
Redo Hunt — clears only the Ten Lines paste box so you can keep hunting the same target without resetting the hunt.
Clear Form — clears the current paste and form fields, but does not delete your saved logs.
Clear All — wipes the current entry and the Active Hunt Log for a completely clean slate, but keeps Shiny History.
Did It Shine? — use this when you got the shiny. It saves a shiny result based on your current hunting target, asks for the final calibrations, moves it to the Shiny History tab, and resets the active hunt log.
